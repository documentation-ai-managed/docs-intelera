## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An active email address for account verification
- At least 2 GB of free disk space for Mobirise installation

<Callout kind="info">
  Intelera integrates seamlessly with Mobirise, a no-code website builder. Download it from the official site after account creation.
</Callout>

## Create Your Intelera Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes to access your dashboard and project tools.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://app.intelera.ai` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and select **Small Business** as your use case.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and access your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Download and Install Mobirise

Mobirise powers your Intelera projects with drag-and-drop design.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="macOS" icon="apple">
    Download the installer from `https://mobirise.com/download/macos.dmg`.

    ```bash
    # Mount the DMG and drag to Applications
    hdiutil attach mobirise.dmg
    open /Volumes/Mobirise/Mobirise.app
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Windows" icon="monitor">
    Get the EXE from `https://mobirise.com/download/windows.exe` and run as administrator.
    
    ```bash
    # Via PowerShell (optional)
    Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "https://mobirise.com/download/windows.exe" -OutFile "Mobirise.exe"
    .\Mobirise.exe
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Linux" icon="linux">
    Use the AppImage from `https://mobirise.com/download/linux.AppImage`.
    
    ```bash
    chmod +x Mobirise.AppImage
    ./Mobirise.AppImage
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Link your Intelera account in Mobirise via **Extensions > Intelera Integration** after installation.
</Callout>

## Launch Your First Website Project

Create a professional site for your small business quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Mobirise" icon="play">
    Launch Mobirise and select **New Site > Business Template**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Intelera" icon="link">
    Go to **Extensions**, search for Intelera, and authenticate with your `YOUR_API_KEY` from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Blocks" icon="edit-3">
    Drag blocks for hero, services, and contact. Preview with **Preview** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish to Intelera" icon="upload-cloud">
    Click **Publish > Intelera Hosting** to deploy live at `https://your-site.intelera.ai`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
