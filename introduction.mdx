## Overview

Intelera.ai empowers small businesses to build stunning, professional websites without coding expertise. You leverage intuitive tools like Mobirise to drag-and-drop elements, customize designs, and launch sites quickly. Our platform focuses on simplicity, speed, and results, helping you establish a strong online presence tailored to your brand.

## Quick Start

Get your first website live in minutes. Follow these steps to download, build, and deploy.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Mobirise" icon="download">
    Visit the official Mobirise site and install the app for your platform.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="macOS" icon="apple">
        Download from the Mac App Store or direct link.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Windows" icon="monitor">
        Get the installer from the Mobirise website.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Project" icon="plus">
    Launch Mobirise, select a template, and start adding blocks like headers and galleries.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize and Publish" icon="rocket">
    Edit colors (try our brand blue `#3B82F6`), preview, then publish to Intelera hosting.
  </Step>
</Steps>
