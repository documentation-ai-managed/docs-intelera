## Overview

To get started with Intelera, sign up and download the Mobirise-based builder.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://intelera.ai/signup`
2. Create a free account
3. Download the Intelera Web Builder
4. Install the app on your Mac or PC
5. Launch the app and log in with your Intelera credentials

## Tip

<Callout kind="tip">
Explore starter templates inside the builder to save time.
</Callout>
