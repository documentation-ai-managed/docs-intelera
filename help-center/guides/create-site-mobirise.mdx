## Build Your First Site

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Intelera Web Builder">
    Log in and select "New Site."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Template">
    Browse templates and click one to start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag & drop blocks">
    From the left panel, drag blocks (header, images, text) onto your page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview your site">
    Click "Preview" to see your live site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Hit "Publish" and follow the prompts to go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
