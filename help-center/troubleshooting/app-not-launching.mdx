---
title: App Not Launching
description: Fix Intelera Web Builder launch issues.
---

## Symptoms
- Intelera app won't open
- Splash screen appears and disappears

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Restart your computer">
    This resolves temporary conflicts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reinstall the app">
    Uninstall Intelera, then re-download from `intelera.ai/download` and install.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check system requirements">
    Make sure your device meets Intelera's minimum specs (macOS 11+, Windows 10+).
  </Step>
</Steps>
